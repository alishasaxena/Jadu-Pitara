Micro-improvement Repository

This micro-improvement repository includes projects under various domains of school improvement (adapted from Shala Siddhi Framework, SQAA and PM SHRI SQAF) . These domains help categorize different improvement journeys a school must lead to (directly or indirectly) provide meaningful learning experiences for children.  These projects can be taken up by school leaders, teachers, education NGOs, system leaders and others. ,
